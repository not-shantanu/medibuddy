The MediBuddy Home Screen Redesign aims to address the needs of urban Indian users by making healthcare services more accessible, intuitive, and business-aligned. This case study explores the challenges with the current UI and presents a user-centered solution that balances business priorities with real user pain points.

Problem with Current UI

🛈
Information Overload
With 10 distinct priority elements specified by the product team, users face cognitive load challenges navigating the interface.
☰
Prioritization Disconnects
Business prioritization may not align with user mental models, creating friction in service discovery.
👤
Personalization Limitation
While personalization is priority #2, effective implementation requires sophisticated user behavior analysis.
↖
Vertical Real Estate
Users typically don't scroll beyond 2-3 screens, yet the priority list suggests content extends beyond this threshold.

Market Insights & Revenue Sources

According to market research, the Indian diagnostics and health-tech market is rapidly growing, with pathology and eDiagnostics leading in market share. Revenue is primarily driven by high-frequency, high-revenue services (P0), while high-ticket, low-frequency services like Surgery Care require strategic placement for growth.

🩺 Service 📊 User Frequency 💰 Revenue Contribution 🎯 Strategic Priority
🩺 Talk to Doctor High (65%) 38% P0
🧪 Book Lab Test Medium (47%) 29% P0
💊 Buy Medicines High (72%) 41% P0
🏥 Surgery Care Low (9%) 18% P2
🦷 Vision/Dental Medium (23%) 12% P1
